Street Yak Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Street Yak connects people with their local communities. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our mobile application.

Data We Collect

  • Account information: email address, username, and password (stored as a secure hash — never in plain text)
  • Location data: postcode and home address, used solely to assign you to the correct local communities
  • Social login: if you sign in via Facebook, Google, or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from that provider
  • Content: posts, comments, and media you voluntarily submit to the platform
  • Device tokens: used only to deliver push notifications you have opted into

How We Use Your Data

  • To assign you to the correct geographic communities based on your address
  • To display your posts and comments to other verified local users
  • To send you relevant community notifications (only if you enable them)
  • To authenticate your account and keep your session secure

What We Never Do

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your address for marketing or advertising profiling
  • Share your exact home address with other users (only general area such as village or town is ever shown)
  • Use your data for any purpose beyond operating the Street Yak service

Third-Party Services

Street Yak uses the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Facebook Login — Facebook Data Policy
  • Google Sign-In — Google Privacy Policy
  • Sign in with Apple — Apple Privacy Policy

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. See our data deletion instructions.

Security

Passwords are hashed using BCrypt. All API communication uses authenticated tokens. Location data is stored in a secured database accessible only to the application.
